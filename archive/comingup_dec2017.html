Club Christmas Dinner - 7th December

This year's annual dinner will be held at Red Bar & Lounge at 45 Dene Street, Dorking, RH4 2DW. Please aim to arrive from 7pm, ready to be seated at 7:30pm. Allow plenty of time to find parking as it is also the Dorking Gala Evening on 7th December. Traffic through Dorking will be for access to parking only - see this map for details.

Dene Street is a North-bound one way street, and you cannot turn off the High Street into it. The easiest approach by car is to turn off the bypass down Chart Lane, which becomes Dene Street; the restaurant is then on the left opposite some public parking spaces. There are also some spaces in front of the restaurant itself.
Alternatively, you can use one of the other car parks in Dorking and walk up Dene Street a hundred yards or so from the High Street. Please see this Google Street View image showing the restaurant on the left.
23 people have booked to attend and the restaurant has been given the food order. If you find you are unable to come, it is important you let Roger Humphries know as soon as possible.
We look forward to seeing you at this very enjoyable event.
