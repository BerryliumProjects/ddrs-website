In August we don't have a Tuesday evening meeting, to avoid the traditional holiday season. However we meet at a member's home for a summer social with a fish and chips supper.

Partners are of course invited too!

In December we enjoy a Christmas dinner at a local restaurant, again with partners. An opportunity to celebrate and share the personal and club achievements of the year. Ex-members (associates) who are able to attend are also warmly invited to renew friendships.

The Society celebrates its seventieth anniversary in 2017, and a special commemorative lunch on 29th April at Denbies Vineyard marked the occasion. The invitation was extended to include local and RSGB dignatories, ex-members and other local clubs.
