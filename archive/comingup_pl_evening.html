VHF evening at Polesden Lacey - 3rd July

Members of the club will be out operating /P on TUESDAY 3rd July from Polesden Lacey overflow car park, 6pm to 10:30pm, using the club callsign G7DOR.

  • 6pm - 7pm : 2 metres FM.
  • 7pm - 10:30pm : 2 metres SSB

Please contact us, or come along in person.

On Saturday 7 July, if there is any support, the club will participate in a VHF Field day from 3pm at the same location. Please contact John G3YGG if you are interested.
