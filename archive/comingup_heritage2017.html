Heritage Open Day - 9 September 2017

Polesden Lacey House and Grounds (Great Bookham, near Dorking, Surrey, RH5 6BD) is holding a Heritage Open Day on Saturday 9 September. The Club will be operating from there from about 10am to 5pm on a number of bands including 2m. On this day admission is free to the house and grounds and a considerable number of people are expected to attend.

Setting up will commence at 8:30am and anyone who is able to attend and offer assistance and support would be most welcome, particularly during the setting up period. More details can be obtained from Roger Humphreys.
