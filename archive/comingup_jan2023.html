The story of Jodrell Bank - 24th January 2023

Professor Ian Morison, G0DMU

This is a recorded lecture from the RSGB, delivered via Zoom rather than in-person. Members should receive a link to the meeting by email, which you can join from 7:45pm.

Professor Morison joined the University of Manchester's Jodrell Bank Observatory as a research student in 1965, initially working on data acquisition systems for the observatory's instruments. He became a staff member in 1970 and went on to play a key role in the development of MERLIN, an array of radio telescopes with a high resolution in the radio spectrum.
