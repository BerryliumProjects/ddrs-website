At certain times, the club is able to make use of a fixed amateur radio station (callsign M0ESG) at the 7th Epsom Scout headquarters, which is behind the Epsom Methodist Church Hall, Ashley Road, Epsom, KT18 5AQ.

There is a fixed HF dipole, a rotatable beam and 2m vertical antenna available, with a fixed HF rig and space for several other operating positions. Training courses are held here, where hands-on experience can be gained.

Each October, the station usually takes part in the annual Jamboree on the Air, which the club supports by providing experienced qualified operators, who are available to answer questions and enable members of all the Scout sections to participate. Unfortunately this particular station's involvement has had to be cancelled for 2017, however Scouts can still join in over the internet.
