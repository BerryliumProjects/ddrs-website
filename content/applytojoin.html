Apply to join the Club

You can visit one of our in-person meetings to meet us and find out more about our activities. We have a paper form available for visitors to register with us and receive information about events by email. If you would like to participate in more than a couple of meetings as a visitor, we ask that you join us as a paid-up member. Please refer to our Constitution page for details.

If you have an email program on your computer or device, you can click here to generate a form which you can fill out to submit your details. If that doesn't work, simply contact us at "ddrs.secretary@gmail.com" and we will be in touch.
