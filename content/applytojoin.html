Apply to join the Club

You can visit one of our in-person meetings to meet us and find out more about our activities. We have a paper form available for visitors to register with us and receive information about events by email. If you would like to participate in more than a couple of meetings as a visitor, we ask that you join us as a paid-up member. Please refer to our Constitution page for details.

If you use a locally installed email program on your computer or device, you can click here to generate a form which you can fill out to submit your details. If you access email through your browser, you can copy and paste the form below into a new email, fill it in and send it to us.

To:      ddrs.secretary@gmail.com
Subject: DDRS Membership Application

Dear DDRS secretary,

I would like to join the Dorking and District Radio Society for the current year (which runs from 1st November), and agree to abide by the club's rules as set out at http://www.ddrs.org.uk/constitution.html.
