We hold regular club nets on-air on Sunday mornings from 8:15am on 7.150MHz (LSB) or 3.772MHz (LSB). Members also often get together informally on other days around 10am on or near 144.775 MHz (FM). Our main club callsign is G7DOR, and we also have the original G3CZU. If you don't have a suitable receiver yourself, you could try listening in on a WebSDR (receiver connected to the Internet):

  • Watford (G3VHH) (VHF) (South Hertfordshire)
  • Rugeley (HF) (Staffordshire)
  • Hack Green (HF) (Cheshire)
  • Kelvedon Hatch (GB0SNB) (HF) (Essex)
  • Kelvedon Hatch (GB0SNB) (VHF) (Essex)

For further details on our activities, including membership enquiries and training opportunities to obtain a licence, please email the club secretary Susan Ellinor (M6XUV), at ddrs.secretary@gmail.com.

Alternatively, just turn up and introduce yourself at one of our regular topical meetings, usually held on the 4th Tuesday of the month. Visitors are always welcome.
