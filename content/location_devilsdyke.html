South Downs Expedition 2017

A good time was had by all who attended this club event on a fine and warm evening. The rig consisted of the Club's IC7400 100W transceiver, running from a generator via a mains PSU; the antenna was a short boom, 5 element Yagi at 20ft above ground pointing north-west. Our operating location was from the end of the Devil's Dyke National Trust car park (IO90VV) which is 700ft above sea level on the South Downs near Brighton.

As usual we were interested in chatting rather than "contesting" and the Dorking Club was complimented on putting on a /P station for that purpose. Thanks to all members who helped out at the QTH and on the air.

Club members attending were: Jon G0GNA, Tony G4XYC, Dennis G4EYB, Mike 2E0ESU, Keith 2E0GBK and Roger M3FZE, we were also joined by Phil G4UDU from Findon.
