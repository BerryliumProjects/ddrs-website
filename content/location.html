We have a programme of monthly evening meetings, which are held at the Friends Meeting House, Butter Hill (off South Street), Dorking starting at 7:45 pm. Please see our calendar for details. Parking is available behind Sainsbury's on the South side of the High Street, then walk under the "arch" on Rose Hill; or in a car park further along South Street, on the left.

We help to support a 7th Epsom Scout Station and Shack, M0ESG. We also operate from this base when supporting the annual Jamboree on the Air (JOTA), welcoming members of local Scout groups to an open radio station they can participate in.

We have privileged arrangements with the National Trust to operate occasionally from some of their local properties: Ranmore Common, Headley Heath and Leith Hill.

In the summer, we usually have an expedition to the Devil's Dyke on the South Downs, an elevated rural location.
