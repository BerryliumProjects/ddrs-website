In the summer months, the club typically plans to meet at outside venues rather than having traditional meetings with a speaker. This could include operating evenings (incorporating a pub meal afterwards) or a fish and chips party.

In December we enjoy a Christmas dinner at a local restaurant, again with partners. An opportunity to celebrate and share the personal and club achievements of the year. Ex‑members (associates) who are able to attend are also warmly invited to renew friendships.

The Society celebrated its seventieth anniversary in 2017, and a special commemorative lunch on 29th April at Denbies Vineyard marked the occasion. The invitation was extended to include local and RSGB dignatories, ex‑members and other local clubs.
