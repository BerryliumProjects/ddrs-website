Meetings Cancelled

It is with regret that we must postpone Club meetings - March, April and maybe further on in the year, in light of government announcements concerning the spread of the COVID-19 virus.

Don't forget that you can keep in touch with DDRS either through this website or on the air:

  • 80m - Sundays at 8:30am, 3772 KHz
  • 2m - around 10:00am daily, 144.775 MHz

The Radio Society of Great Britain is currently carrying a news article regarding the impact of Coronavirus precautions on other events in the wider amateur radio world. No doubt this will be maintained with ongoing news.

Other local amateur radio clubs may also be holding regular Nets on the air, which may help keep you informed about what is going on in the area.
