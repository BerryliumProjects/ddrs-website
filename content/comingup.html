22 August 2023 - Outdoor Operating Evening

Denbies Hillside, Ranmore Common

Our next meeting is a planned outdoor operating event (subject to weather conditions).

This is a National Trust location with a car park and spectacular views - see map for address and directions. The grid square locator is IO91TF.

Access to the main field (with the best views) is through a gate next to the ticket machine and display board. It is light until around 20:15 so we suggest you come from 18:30 onwards to set up equipment.

Please remember that this is a public site so give consideration to others using the location for recreation. Ideally, please bring headphones to avoid turning the speaker volume up too much.

Some club equipment will be brought to site for people to use. It is intended to operate as many bands a possible, but also to share the operating particularly to give some who have limited access to some bands at home a chance to try other bands and modes.

Club members are welcome to bring their own kit particularly if you are operating a mode such as digital or one of the lesser used bands and are willing to share / demonstrate the kit to other club members. If possible you should try to arrive with the idea of coordinating bands so as many as possible get a chance to try a band.

Please note there are no toilet facilities or mains power at the site, though it may be possible to bring a generator to site which will support medium power equipment.
