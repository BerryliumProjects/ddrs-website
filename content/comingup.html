Annual General Meeting with RSGB Film - 27 November 2018

The forthcoming meeting on Tuesday 27 November will consist of the AGM followed by a video entitled "The K3LR Super Station", which comes from the 2018 RSGB Convention and lasts approximately 40 minutes.

There are various matters to discuss at the AGM, including the election of officers and club sales.

Please also note that menu choices for the Christmas meal are also needed by the AGM, with new payment arrangements this year.
