Saturday 14th September - Special Event Station

At Christ Church, Epsom Common

The club will be setting up a station using the special callsign GB4CEC, as part of the annual Churches and Chapels on the Air national event, in the grounds of Christ Church, Epsom Common Road, Epsom, KT19 8NE. This is situated at the edge of Epsom Common, which is a Site of Special Scientific Interest and also a Local Nature Reserve. If you are not attending, you could alternatively make contact with the station from home.
