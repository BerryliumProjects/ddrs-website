Tuesday 21st January - History and CW in Essex

Colin Berry, M0GXV

Colin will talk about a visit to the Secret Nuclear Bunker at Kelvedon Hatch in Essex - also the site of the GB0SNB web SDR. The following day was a Boot Camp to improve Morse code skills, organised by the Essex CW Amateur Radio Club, and Colin will explain and demonstrate how that worked.

This is an in-person meeting at Dorking, which is a change to the planned arrangements. The Quaker meeting house is unavailable in February, so will be an ideal opportunity for Karl Read to give his remote presentation.
