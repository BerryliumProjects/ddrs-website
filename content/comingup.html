Top Secret: From Ciphers to Cyber Security - 28th February 2023

Dr Liz Bruton

This will be a talk about the history of signals intelligence from the First World War to the present day, based on the exhibition of the same name that the speaker curated at the Science Museum, London in 2019-2022.

The meeting will be over Zoom rather than in-person. Members should receive a link by email.
