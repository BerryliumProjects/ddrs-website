Virtual End of Year Social
22 December 2020

At this time of the year it would be nice if we could all sit around a table and have a great evening chatting and eating dinner, sadly this year that's not possible.

So the next best thing is for us to join together online. Can we encourage you to join us on Tuesday 22nd December from 7:45pm. Please arrange your own drinks and food or nibbles etc. Silly party hats and crackers are optional.

Members should have received an email with the appropriate link to join the meeting - if yours hasn't arrived, please check your junk/spam folder or contact the club events mailbox to verify your email address with us.

If you can't make the meeting, can we take the opportunity to wish you all a happy Christmas, a healthy 2021 and of course lots of exciting DX !
