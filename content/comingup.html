South Downs evening - 23rd July

The aim of this annual expedition to the Devil's Dyke on the South Downs is to establish and run a 2m SSB/FM station, commencing at 5:30pm and operating until about 8:30pm using G7DOR/P. All are encouraged to participate or to operate other stations on other frequencies.

Please come to Devil's Dyke to participate in whatever way you wish. There is a pub with good food, where we usually have dinner after the station has been taken down. If you are unable to come, you are of course welcome to call the club station.

The postcode is BN1 8YJ. You can get directions to this beautiful location from the National Trust. We usually set up at the North-East end of the car park behind the restaurant.

Take a look at a previous year's expedition to get a flavour of this popular evening.
