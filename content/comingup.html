Thursday 26th March 2026 - Construction Evening

Led by Colin M0GXV

The club has bought 3 regenerative radio receiver kits, and we will be assembling the components onto the PCBs. There will be soldering irons, solder and basic tools available to use, but if you have your own equipment you are welcome to bring it, but please ensure the soldering iron is well protected when not in use. The activity will be shared out as much as possible so everyone has a chance to take part.

This is the first of 2 construction activities - a later one will cover connecting the board up to controls and sockets, and testing them.
